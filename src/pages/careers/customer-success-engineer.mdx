<h2>The role</h2>

As a Customer Success Engineer, you will be a critical part of our growing
company, being the first point of contact with our users, handing their
questions and providing initial responses when possible. This is a technical
role that requires an engineering background. The candidate will directly liaise
with the founders and the engineering team to better handle requests and grow
the team.

<h2>Responsibilities</h2>

- Monitoring developer activity on GitHub (issues and discussions), Slack and
  other channels
- Answering initial responses to support cases
- Guiding users to the relevant information (previous answers, documentation
  etc) or source feedback from the team to understand the problem and help users
- Reproduce issues in a consistent environment and create test cases
- Create a feedback loop to monitor progress and ensure complete coverage of
  issues
- Improve the main KPIs 'time to response' and 'time to fix', and build a team
  as the number of user requests and issues grows

<h2>Requirements</h2>

- Familiar with Java, C++, and SQL
- Experience working with GitHub source code management, and CI/CD tools
- Excellent written and communication skills
- Strong team player who is open to guiding colleagues on your areas of
  expertise
- The ability to understand, shape and deliver against customers' requirements
