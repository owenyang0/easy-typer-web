<h2>The role</h2>

Helping developers solve their problems is at the center of what we do. Your
role will consist of engaging with the user base and orchestrating the growth of
our open source communities.

We are looking for an individual who is passionate about data and driven to spur
continuous awareness of a deep-technology product. We recognize the value of an
enthusiastic and energetic individual who is excited about interacting with
developer communities on a daily basis.

This is an open-ended role with a substantial degree of autonomy. You will own
the development of outreach channels and partnerships to spur adoption. In
addition to planning and participating in events, webinars, podcasts, social
media or any other initiative to grow the awareness and presence of QuestDB in
open source communities.

<h2>Responsibilities</h2>

- Represent QuestDB at technical conferences and events to build our reputation
  in relevant developer-led communities
- Own and improve the developer onboarding experience by building demos,
  tutorials, and workshops
- Identify products, channels, and platforms as strategic partners to create
  initiatives to grow the developer community and product awareness
- Work closely with product, content, and core engineering teams to continuously
  improve DevRel initiatives leveraging tools and methodologies
- Be the voice of QuestDB's developer community and proactively channel our
  community's interests and experiences back to the engineering team

<h2>Requirements</h2>

- Experience working in Developer Relations or as a Software Engineer for 2+
  years
- Proficient in at least one common programming language and comfortable working
  with databases
- Passionate about open source and a strong commitment to the ecosystem
- Entrepreneurial mindset
- Excellent spoken and written English
- Familiarity working with time series databases and SQL is a plus
- Experience in community development and relationships in open source
  communities is a plus
- Familiarity with cloud-native technologies and observability tools is a plus

We're passionate about building a diverse team of people driven to solve the
most challenging problems. If you don't tick all the boxes but think you can
bring something exceptional to the table - apply anyway.
